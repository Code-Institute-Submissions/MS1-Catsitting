  • Bowls cleaned
  • Food refilled and water refreshed
  • Clean litter tray
  • Grooming
  • Lots of cuddles and play
  • Administration of medicine if required

About me

My name is Sian and I am a qualified veterinary nurse with extensive knowledge and experience of cats. I have volunteered with many animal charities such as the Mayhew and Battersea home for cats and dogs, helping nursing and hosting cats to help them find their forever homes. I am also the proud human of Elmer.

  • Feline First Aid qualified
  • DBS checked
  • Fully insured
